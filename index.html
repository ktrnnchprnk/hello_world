About

Exeter, UK

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Living Systems Institute, University of Exeter. My research integrates theory with experimental data to identify organising principles of neural and neuroendocrine dynamics, with a particular interest in state transitions and their physiological relevance.

I am currently part of the CircadiAgeing project, where I use mathematical modelling to investigate how circadian rhythms regulate physiological processes and contribute to healthy ageing. This work aims to uncover mechanistic links between biological timing systems and age-related changes in health.
