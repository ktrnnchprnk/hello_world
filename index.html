Kateryna (Kate) Nechyporenko

PhD Researcher in Mathematics | Living Systems Institute, University of Exeter

About Me

I am doing a PhD at University of Exeter. My primary research interests are mathematical modelling of neuroendocrine systems and nonlinear dynamics.
